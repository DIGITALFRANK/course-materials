A plane in $\mathbb{R}^4$ is spanned by the two vectors 
\[ #1  \text{ and } #2 . \]
$A$ is the matrix whose columns are these vectors.


\prob{1} (20 pts) \\
Give the projection matrix $P$ that projects a vector $b \in \mathbb{R}^4$ onto this plane.

\[ b = #3 ? \]
Verify that the error and projection vectors $e$ and $p$ exist in the correct subspaces generated by $A$, and that they are orthogonal.
